  • 2/1/13 Week-1

    At these day, our team was formally established. BIT-China for one green earth is our slogan .And what we want to do is to improve the optimistic temperature from 37℃ to 42℃ in fermentation of E.coli ,which can devote to increasing the competitive of fermentative products with many aspects, including cut down consumption of energy of source, simplifies and advances the efficiency of whole biological productive process.

  • 3/1/13 Week-1

    We design two systems to achieve our great goal. The first system is the customize thermotolerance system, in order to improve the tolerance to heat, we choose heat shock protein family (HSP) with the controlling of RAN thermometer. Another system is intelligent quorum regulating system, we use quorum sensing device and programmed cell death(PCD) device to control the cell density ,targeting at maintaining cell in high productive with an optimistic cell number.
